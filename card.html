Tiger

Tiger hunt in water and can swim really well. Mom tigers give swimming lesson to their cubs.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 8.2 – 13 ft. (Male, Adult), 6.6 – 9 ft. (Female, Adult)
  • Average Lifespan: 8 – 10 years (In the wild)
  • Habitat: Rain forests, Grasslands, Savannas and Even Mangrove Swamps

Tiger is the largest living cat species and one of the endangered animals. Male Tiger have slightly long tail than female. Tiger marks it territory by spraying urine and it can last upto 40 days.
